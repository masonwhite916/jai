Frequently Asked Questions

What areas does JAI Roadside Assistance cover?
JAI currently serves Riyadh and its surrounding areas, with more cities coming soon across Saudi Arabia.
Is JAI Roadside Assistance available 24/7?
Yes. JAI operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including holidays. Our nearest unit is dispatched instantly when you need help.
How much does a JAI membership cost?
JAI offers three annual membership plans, all including VAT: Basic at 199 SAR/year, Accidents at 299 SAR/year, and Rental at 600 SAR/year.
What services does JAI provide?
JAI provides battery charge (jump-start), emergency towing, fuel supply, tire change, lockout service, light mechanical repair, and computer diagnostics.
How do I contact JAI Roadside Assistance?
You can reach JAI by phone or WhatsApp at +966 55 561 6449, available 24/7.
